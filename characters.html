My picks for top 7 Cartoon Characters

The following are the 7 cartoon characters I like the most. By personality, and by looks. Some of them will come in pairs, because without eachother, they won't be great as they are.

7: Snoopy and Woodstock

Snoopy is the Beagle that appears in the comic strips written by Charles.M.Schulz. Snoopy was inspired by Spike, one of Schulz's chiclhood dogs. The other main characters are Snoopy's owner Charlie Brown, and Woodstock the bird, who is Snoopy's best friend and side kick. He was named after the Woodstock festival of 1969. In the comics, most of them have 8 boxes, and they tell the whole story of each scene.

6: Donald Duck, MIckey Mouse, Goofy and Co..

Donald Duck is a duck that was made by Walt Disney, who wears a sailor shirt and cap with a bow tie. Mickey Mouse is the most popular character of Walt Disney's animated cartoons. Mickey is often presented as a cheerful and mischievous mouse. Goofy is a tall, dog who wears a turtle neck and vest, with pants, shoes, white gloves, and a tall hat. Goofy is a close friend of Mickey Mouse and Donald Duck.

5:Daffy Duck

Daffy Duck is a character created by the Warner Bros. Daffy is a black duck, and has usually been depicted as a foil for Bugs Bunny. Dafffy's catchphrase is "Youuu're deththpicable!"

4: Popeye

Popeye is a sailor who possesses superhuman strength after ingesting an always-handy can of spinach, and always seen with a pipe in his mouth. He normally swallows the spinach, or sucks it with his pipe. Popeye's enemy is Bluto, the big bearded muscley bully who mainly tries to win Olive Oyl from Popeye.

3: Bugs Bunny and Elmer Fudd

Bugs Bunny and Elmer Fudd are characters from the Loony Tunes and Merrie Melodies cartoons. Elmer tries to shoot Bugs Bunny, which he does in the ends. Most of the time, Bugs outsmarts Elmer Fudd, and the only preson who has outsmarted Bugs is Cecil the turtle. I like Bugs because he seems pretty chill and is pretty cunning and smart.

2: Pink Panther

The pink panther, is a pink panther, and represents a large, valuable pink diamond in the movie The Pink Panther. I like him because of his looks as well as his funny and silly personality.
